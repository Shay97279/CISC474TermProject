About



Purpose


Our purpose of creating this web app is to assist people with tracking their finances. It is directed towards those learning the fundamentals of finances or anyone who would like to better understand their finances. For that reason, we created this simple and comprehensive financial app to track expenses, income, and assets. Our goal is to not only assist with understanding this information, but to be able to use it to better one's financial standings. For this reason we created tools such as to project ones statistics based on targets and time. In addition, we created a page to assist those with learning about the fundamentals of personal finance. We hope you find our app useful!

Vision

Our vision for this application is to further add value to any individual tracking their finances.
Our development roadmap includes features such as:

  1. Scanning and uploading documents to autofill information. This would include being able to upload paystubs, checks, bank statements, and etc instead of having to manually add financial info.
  2. Email notifications to summarize a user's finances monthly. This would also involve reminders to add certain financial information if necessary.
  3. Community Forum. This would add the ability to discuss finances, and perhaps learn from others. We recommend each individual to make their own informed financial decisions.
  4. Connect application to bank. This would allow for automated information input.
